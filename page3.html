Featured Youtuber

Mark "Markiplier" Edward Fischbach

Markiplier is an iconic content creator on Youtube. The usual content he uploads

are playthroughs of indie visual novels or horror games, his most popular content

being dedicated to the indie horror survival game, "Five Nights at Freddy's".

He is also a film maker and actor, his projects are usually interactive and engaging

movies via Youtube and currently has an ongoing project for another film,

"Iron Lung", which has fans on the edge of their seat.
